Safety Guidelines & Emergency Support

In Case of Immediate Danger

Emergency Contacts

  • Police Emergency: 911
  • GBV Helpline: 1-800-HELP-GBV
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • National Suicide Prevention: 988

Safety Steps

  1. Get to a safe location immediately
  2. Call emergency services if in immediate danger
  3. Preserve evidence if possible
  4. Reach out to trusted friends or family
  5. Contact support organizations
