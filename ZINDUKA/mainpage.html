ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

Zinduka is a movement dedicated to ending femicide and all forms of violence against women and girls. We aim to do awaken communities to speak out, take action, and protect lives. We raise awareness, support survivors, and push for change through education, advocacy, and digital campaigns. At Zinduka, we believe every woman deserves to live free from fear.

MISSION AND VISION

OUR MISSION

"Empowering individuals to end femicide and GBV through safe reporting, awareness and support"

OUR VISION

"A Kenya free from femicide and gender-basedviolence, where every woman and girl, regardless of age and background, feels safe, empowered and heard!"
