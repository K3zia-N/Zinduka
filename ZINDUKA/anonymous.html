Her Voice, Her Power

For years I struggled with depression in silence. No one could see it on my face. It was heavy... unbearable. But writing this is proof: I made it through.

Sharing my story was the first step to healing. I want you to know — you are not alone. Speak, write, cry, shout — do what you must to reclaim your light.
