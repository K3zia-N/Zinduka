Welcome

This project is dedicated to understanding and combating femicide through the collection, analysis, and sharing of data and stories. Explore our findings, read case studies, and join the call for justice and change.

About the Project

The Femicide Project investigates patterns, causes, and policy responses to the gender-based killing of women and girls. We aim to amplify voices, influence policy, and drive social change.
