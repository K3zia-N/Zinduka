What Is Zinduka?

ABOUT US

Our digital platform,Zinduka, is a passionate movement committed to eradicating femicide and gender-based violence (GBV) in Kenya.Our mission is to raise awareness, empower survivors, and drive systemic change through advocacy, education, and community engagement. We believe in a Kenya where every person—regardless of gender—can live free from fear, violence, and discrimination, and we are calling on all Kenyans to rise, speak out, and take action against GBV. Together, we can build a safer, more just society for all.

WHAT IS FEMICIDE?

This is the intentional killing of women on the account of their gender. It manifests in various forms but the common one is through Gender-Based Violence.
